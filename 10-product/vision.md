# Vision

## North star

YFYT (Your First Year Trading) is an AI trading co-pilot that teaches beginners to think like traders — not by lecturing, but by analysing real markets alongside them, explaining every decision, and enforcing discipline through structured risk management.

## Why now

- Multimodal AI (Claude Opus 4.6) is genuinely good enough to analyse charts, reason about price action, and explain concepts — this wasn't possible in 2022
- No product bridges the gap between "too simple" (Robinhood) and "too complex" (TradingView) with an AI education layer
- Anti and Elsh want to learn trading themselves — direct dogfood users from day one

## Success (12 months)

- Consistently profitable paper trading for 3+ months (2%/week from £10K pot)
- Wingman surfaces behavioural patterns in our trading that we didn't know — and is right
- The system runs autonomously: morning scans, signals, alerts, post-mortems — minimal manual intervention
- We feel confident enough to start real-money trading with discipline baked in

## Non-goals

- Not a trading signal/bot platform — it explains reasoning, doesn't just say "buy"
- Not a broker — no real money in the app (paper trading only for now)
- Not a passive course — no video lectures, no quizzes without context
- Not a social/copy trading platform — individual learning journey
- Not for day traders needing sub-second execution or algo traders writing Python strategies
- Not for crypto — stocks/ETFs only, simpler regulatory position
