# Users

## Primary users

**Anti** — FAANG software engineer, London (GMT/BST). PM/founder mode — directs AI agents (Claude Code) to build. Complete trading beginner. Wants to learn by doing, not by watching.

**Elsh** — Anti's girlfriend. Software engineer, New York (EST, 5 hours behind London). Co-building YFYT as of April 2026. Also a trading beginner.

Both are the direct dogfood users — every design decision is validated against their experience.

### The broader persona — "The Serious Beginner"

- Age 22–35, professional, has disposable income (£500–£5,000 to start)
- Has read a book or two on investing but zero actual trading experience
- Deeply uncomfortable with the gap between "I think this stock might go up" and "I understand what I'm doing"
- Time-constrained — can't spend 4 hours a day watching charts
- Motivated by long-term wealth building, not get-rich-quick schemes

## Secondary / adjacent

**"The Rebuilding Trader"** — has tried trading before, lost money, doesn't know exactly why. Wants structure and discipline, not more signals.

## Anti-personas

- Day traders needing sub-second execution
- Algo traders who want to write strategies in Python
- Professional portfolio managers
- Crypto degens chasing 10x plays
- 16-year-olds who discovered crypto on TikTok (want signals, will churn)
