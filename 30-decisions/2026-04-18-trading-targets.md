# Decision: Paper Trading Targets

**Date:** April 18, 2026  
**Decided by:** Anti + Claude

## Decision

- Paper trading pot: **£10,000**
- Weekly target: **2% (~£200/week)**
- Validation period: **3 months minimum** of consistent paper profit before considering real money
- Max 1-3 trades per day

## Context

Anti initially proposed £200-1K/week from a £5K pot (4-20% weekly). After analysis:
- 4%/week would outperform every hedge fund on earth
- 20%/week is 1,000%+ annualised — unrealistic
- Revised to £10K pot with 2%/week target — aggressive but achievable for disciplined swing traders

## Why this matters

All position sizing, risk rules, and P&L tracking should be built around a £10K starting balance. The risk engine caps individual trade risk at 2% of portfolio (£200 max loss per trade), which aligns with the weekly gain target — one bad trade shouldn't wipe a week's gains.
